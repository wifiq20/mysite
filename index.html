Why is keyword research so important in SEO ?

Keyword research is the first and most important stage of every SEO project.
If you are a SEO newbie, you may be wondering... What is a keyword ?.
A keyword is any phrase you would like your site to rank for in google search results.
A good keyword is a combination of words.In most cases, informational and transactional keywords are the good keyword for most SEO projects.
Before you can find keywords with loads of traffic in Google, you must first develop a list of potential keywords relevant to your business.
First, sketch out a grid of words your target customer might use, then combine them into one big list.With this strategy you can build up a massive list of relevant keywords.
